Steven Nicholas Smith

Steven Nicholas Smith is a filmmaker, photographer, and artist. He was born in Seoul, South Korea, but moved to the U.S. at an early age. Being the son of a Military father, he moved around a lot, until they settled down in West Chester, a suburb of Philadelphia, PA.

Steven was always interested in the arts, starting with drawing and painting as a young kid. As a teenager, he took interest in Hip Hop Dance culture and ventured into the world of B-Boyin', better known as break-dancing. It was his involvement in the dance and Hip-Hop culture that saw him transition from the movement-arts to making moving-images.

When Steven graduated high school, he moved to Philadelphia and attended Drexel University, where he graduated with a Bachelor's in Science, majoring in Digital Media, with a minor in Film & Video. After spending several years in Philadelphia, Steven packed up his things and headed west, to the City of Angels. Armed with ambition and a dream, he continues to pursue his love of the art of storytelling.

He currently works and resides in Los Angeles, CA.
